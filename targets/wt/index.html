The Calculus Lessons give a truly interactive introduction to the ideas of calculus. The first three chapters are entirely geometric. They require only familiarity with functions in the $(x,y)$ plane and an understanding of ratios and rates. They are a fantastic introduction to the ideas of calculus. The remaining chapters are intended to supplement a standard calculus class.
